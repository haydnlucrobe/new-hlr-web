A few years ago, I was approached and asked to join a team of people striving to revive my school's old magazine from 1966. This was the rebirth of The Martlet (the website for which I also wrote), a student-led magazine, available both online and in print. Oh, it's also won awards nationally- as have I for my work designing it, and several other team members for their amazing contributions.
